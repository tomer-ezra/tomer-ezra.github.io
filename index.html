About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Sapienza University, hosted by Stefano Leonardi. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student at Tel Aviv University and was advised by Michal Feldman. My research interests lie in the border of Computer Science and Economics, focusing on the analysis and design of simple mechanisms and algorithms in limited information settings.


I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science at Tel Aviv University in 2016 and my B.Sc. in Computer Science at The Open University - Israel in 2010.
