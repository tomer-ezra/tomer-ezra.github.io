About

I am a Sloan postdoctoral fellow at SLMath. Before that, I was a postdoc at Sapienza University of Rome, hosted by Stefano Leonardi. I received my PhD from Tel Aviv University where I was advised by Michal Feldman. My research interests lie in the border of Computer Science and Economics, focusing on the analysis and design of simple mechanisms and algorithms in limited information settings.
