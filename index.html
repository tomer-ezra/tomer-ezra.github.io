About

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Statistics and Operations Research, School of Mathematical Sciences at Tel Aviv University.
Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Center of Mathematical Sciences and Applications (CMSA) at Harvard University, mentored by Yannai A. Gonczarowski.
I was also the Sloan Postdoctoral Fellow at the Simons Laufer Mathematical Sciences Institute (SLMath), where I took part in the semester program on Mathematics and Computer Science of Market and Mechanism Design.
Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Sapienza University of Rome, hosted by Stefano Leonardi.

I received my PhD from Tel Aviv University, where I was advised by Michal Feldman. My research lies at the interface of Computer Science and Economics, focusing on the analysis and design of simple mechanisms and algorithms under limited information.
